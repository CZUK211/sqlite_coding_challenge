# Insights

- The top customers spend a lot more than others, which shows a small group of customers drive most of the revenue.

- Electronics had the highest revenue, so it seems like that category is the most valuable for the business.

- Some employees are earning above their department average, which could mean they have more experience or better performance.

- Departments have different average salaries, showing that some roles are paid more than others depending on the department.

- Tampa had the highest number of Gold customers, which could mean it is an important location for loyal customers.

- Overall, the data shows where the business makes the most money and which customers and areas are most valuable.
